Content

Home page

The home page will have a brief introduction to the business and a few images of the products offered. Underneath the product images will be the titles of the products and the prices for each product. Underneath that there will be a text box that will explain how to contact the owner and set up a meeting to discusss how they would like there product, prices and dates for pick up or delivery.

Images for the Home page

Classes

This page will contain two images of the classes that are offered. One cake class and one bread class. Underneath the images will be the titles of the classes and the prices for each class. Underneath that there will be a text box that will explain how to contact the owner and set up a meeting to discusss how they would like their product, prices and dates for pick up or delivery.

Images for the Page 2

About Me

This page will give a brief history of the owner and how she got started in the business. It will also have a image of the owner. There will be information to explain her skillsets and expertise and some information to connect with customers and feel comfortable contacting her to order a product.
